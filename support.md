## Frequently Asked Questions

### Game Center

**Q: How do I connect to Game Center?**
A: Game Center connection is automatic when you first launch the game. If you're having trouble connecting, make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device Settings.

**Q: My achievements aren't showing up.**
A: Please ensure you're signed in to Game Center and have an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try signing out and back into Game Center.

**Q: Can I play without Game Center?**
A: Yes, you can play the game without Game Center, but you won't be able to access leaderboards and achievements.

### In-App Purchases

**Q: How do I purchase Pass or power packs?**
A: Tap on the in-app purchase option in the game menu. The purchase will be processed through your Apple ID.

**Q: I purchased a Pass/power pack but didn't receive it.**
A: Please try restarting the game. If the issue persists, contact us with your purchase receipt (you can find it in your App Store purchase history).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple. Please contact Apple Support or visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.

**Q: What happens to my Pass benefits if I cancel?**
A: You'll continue to have access to Pass benefits until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll need to resubscribe to continue receiving daily power rewards.

### Gameplay

**Q: How do I use power-ups?**
A: Power-ups can be activated during gameplay. Tap on the power-up icon when available to use it.

**Q: How do I get daily power rewards?**
A: With an active Pass subscription, you can claim daily power rewards (5 of each power type) once per day. Simply open the game and claim your daily rewards.

**Q: My game progress was lost.**
A: Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you've lost progress, try restarting the app. If the issue continues, contact us with details about when the progress was lost.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The game crashes or freezes.**
A: Try the following steps:

1. Close the app completely and restart it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have the latest version of the game installed
4. Check that your device has sufficient storage space

**Q: The game is running slowly.**
A: Try closing other apps running in the background. If the issue persists, restart your device.

**Q: I can't install or update the game.**
A: Make sure you have:

- Sufficient storage space on your device
- A stable internet connection
- The latest version of iOS installed
