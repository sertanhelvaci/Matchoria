# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 08.01.2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Matchoria (the "Game"). By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

**Game Center Data:**

- When you use Game Center features, Apple may collect and process certain information related to your Game Center account, including:
  - Your Game Center player ID
  - Achievements and leaderboard data
  - Game progress and statistics

This information is managed by Apple in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We do not have direct access to your personal Game Center information.

**Game Analytics:**

- We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the Game experience, such as:
  - Gameplay statistics (scores, levels completed)
  - Feature usage patterns
  - Technical performance metrics

### Information You Provide

**In-App Purchases:**

- When you make in-app purchases (Pass subscriptions or one-time power packs), Apple processes the transaction through your Apple ID.
- We do not collect or store your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Apple.
- We may receive confirmation of your purchase to enable the purchased features in the Game.

**No Personal Information Required:**

- The Game does not require you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to play.
- We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the Game service
- Enable Game Center features (achievements, leaderboards)
- Process and deliver in-app purchases
- Improve the Game experience and fix technical issues
- Analyze gameplay patterns to enhance game features
